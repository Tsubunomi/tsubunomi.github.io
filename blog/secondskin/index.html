Clothes as a “Second Skin”

School Uniforms or Own Clothes?

Clothes as a “Second Skin”

Today, I want to talk about clothes not as fashion, but as a  second skin.

The core meaning of the question “Which do you want to wear to school, school uniforms or your own clothes? ” is to choosbetween a public second skin and a private second skin.

Wearing school uniforms makes you look “formal and public”. When we wear school uniforms, everyone looks the same. We share the same color, same design, and same rules in our organization. Our face hides inside. School uniforms are safe in your organizations, but sometimes boring.

On the other hand, your own clothes make your skin “private” or make you feel free. When we wear our own clothes, we choose colors and styles based on our feelings. Our clothes show your heart. Own clothes are like a personal diary visible to everyone but only we can understand.

In conclusion, choosing between wearing school uniforms and wearing your own clothes is not important. Maybe the real question is “Do you want your second skin to be public or private?” If you want to feel connected in your organization, you can wear a school uniform. If you want to feel free or want to open your heart, you can wear your own clothes. It depends on you to choose between wearing school uniforms and wearing your own clothes. And If you want both, maybe you can design a school uniform that feels like your own skin.
